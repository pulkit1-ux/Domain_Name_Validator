INTRODUCTION

A whopping 94 percent of survey respondents said their organization had experienced one or more phishing email attacks. Phishing email attacks can be difficult to control because they prey on human nature. Even the most knowledgeable users can be duped by a cleverly constructed phishing email with a spoofed from address. And all it takes is one user clicking on a malicious link or attachment to cause a security breach.
